Normal Skin

Balanced, not too oily or dry — the easy-going skin type.
What to focus on: Maintain balance and prevent future damage.

Routine essentials:
Gentle cleanser and lightweight moisturizer.
Daily broad-spectrum sunscreen (SPF 30+).
Occasional exfoliation with mild acids like lactic or mandelic.

Dry Skin

Feels tight, flaky, or dull? Your skin needs more moisture and protection.
What to focus on: Hydration and barrier support.

Routine essentials:
Cream cleanser + rich moisturizer (ceramides/hyaluronic).
Niacinamide for barrier strength.
Gentle renewal with PHAs.
Sunscreen even in winter.

See Profile

Oily Skin

Shine or larger pores? Oily skin produces excess sebum but still needs hydration.
What to focus on: Balance oil, don’t strip it.

Routine essentials:
Salicylic/tea tree foaming cleanser.
Oil-free moisturizer.
Niacinamide for shine control.
Non-comedogenic SPF.

See Profile

Combination Skin

Oily T-zone, dry elsewhere — very common.
What to focus on: Treat zones differently.

Routine essentials:
Gel cleanser.
Lightweight but hydrating moisturizer.
Gentle AHA/BHA exfoliation 2× weekly.
Daily SPF.

See Profile

Sensitive Skin

Easily irritated or reactive.
What to focus on: Calm + barrier repair.

Routine essentials:
Fragrance-free cleanser.
Ceramide + niacinamide moisturizer.
Avoid harsh acids.
Mineral SPF (zinc/titanium).

General Tips & Tricks

Simplify. Cleanse, moisturize, protect.
Be consistent. Results come from habits.
Damp skin. Locks hydration better.
Never skip SPF. Best anti-aging step.
Introduce slowly. Let skin adjust.
Sleep + water. Your skin shows it.
